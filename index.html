Use Case

Smart Shoe Tracker offers a range of features to enhance your safety:

  • Prevent Injury: Advanced tracking helps in analyzing your walking patterns and provides insights to prevent potential injuries.
  • Smart Beacon: Utilizes smart beacon technology for accurate location tracking and alerts in emergency situations.
  • Anti-Harassment: Built-in sensors can detect unusual movements and activate an alert system to prevent harassment.
  • Quick Support Call: In case of emergencies, initiate a quick support call directly from your Smart Shoe Tracker.
